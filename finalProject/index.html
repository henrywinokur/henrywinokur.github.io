My Final Project
Me and my Dad have both always loved baseball and have gone to many games together throuout my life.
I've always loved the Google Doodle baseball game. So I decided to try and make a game as well.
To start I needed a baseball to move on the screen just like it does in the google doodle game.
The next step was to create a bat and make it swing.
Next we needed to make it that when the bat swings on click it will hit the ball.
With that done we created a alert to alert the player of they hit a single homerun double triple or hit it out of the ball park!
